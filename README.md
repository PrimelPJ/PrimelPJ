## ⭐ Featured Projects

### 🌿 Areej - Islamic Personal Growth App
A free web app built for young Muslims to grow in their deen. Includes a full Quran reader, hadith collections, 99 Names of Allah, dua library, sunnah habit tracker, shukr log, and more. Built as sadaqah jariyah.

- 📗 Full Quran reader with per-ayah bookmarks and font control
- 📜 Browse Bukhari, Muslim, Abu Dawud + 6 more hadith collections
- ✦ 99 Names of Allah with full meanings and descriptions
- 🤲 60+ duas across 13 categories
- 📊 Progress monitor with milestones and reminders
- 🌙 Dark mode, badges, daily challenges, and goals

**Live:** [areej-git-main-primelpj21-3352s-projects.vercel.app](https://areej-git-main-primelpj21-3352s-projects.vercel.app)  
**Repo:** https://github.com/PrimelPJ/areej

### 🏋️ Gym - Personal Gym Tracker (iOS)
A clean, minimal workout tracker built with **SwiftUI** focused on fast logging and simple progress stats.

- 3-tab layout: Home, Workout, Profile
- Streak and weekly activity preview
- Daily summary (Sessions, Sets, Volume) and profile stats
- Dark mode support
